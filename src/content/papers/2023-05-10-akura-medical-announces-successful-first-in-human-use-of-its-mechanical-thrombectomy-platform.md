## L’essentiel

Akura Medical announced today it has initiated its first-in-human clinical study of the Akura mechanical thrombectomy platform. A press release notes that the Akura platform is a low-profile solution designed to easily access and efficiently remove large-volume, mixed-morphology clots, and eliminate the guesswork around the procedure.
