## L’essentiel

Global Heart Hub, an international alliance of heart patient organisations, has published an implementation roadmap aimed at patient organisations, healthcare professionals and advocates, and is intended to facilitate shared decision-making between patients and healthcare providers for the treatment of heart valve disease. Its publication coincides with PCR London Valves 2023 (19–21 November, London, UK).
