## L’essentiel

Dated 11 October 2023, the US Centers for Medicare &#38; Medicaid Services (CMS) recently finalised its decision regarding National Coverage Determination (NCD) 20.7 for carotid artery stenting (CAS), confirming coverage expansion. Previously, patients were only eligible for CAS through clinical trials or if they were high-surgical-risk individuals.
