## L’essentiel

Endologix has announced in a press release that the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has granted approval for the Detour system to treat patients with complex peripheral arterial disease (PAD). Patients with long blockages of the superficial femoral artery (SFA) have had limited treatment options.
