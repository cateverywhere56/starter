## L’essentiel

Vivasure Medical has enrolled the 100th patient in its PATCH clinical study, a multicentre, single-arm, pivotal study evaluating the safety and efficacy of the Vivasure PerQseal closure device system. The US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) granted investigational device exemption (IDE) approval earlier this year to advance the PATCH study forward.
