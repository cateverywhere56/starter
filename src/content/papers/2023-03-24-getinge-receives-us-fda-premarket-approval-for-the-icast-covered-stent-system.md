## L’essentiel

Getinge&#8216;s iCast covered stent system has received premarket approval from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for the treatment of patients with iliac arterial occlusive disease. Iliac arterial occlusive disease is a type of peripheral arterial disease (PAD), which affects 8 million people in the USA.
