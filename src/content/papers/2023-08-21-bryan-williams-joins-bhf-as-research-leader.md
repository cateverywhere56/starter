## L’essentiel

The British Heart Foundation (BHF) has appointed research leader and NHS consultant physician Bryan Williams as its first chief scientific and medical officer. Williams will join the charity’s executive group in Autumn 2023 and succeed Sir Nilesh Samani (medical director) in leading the BHF’s research and medical strategy.
