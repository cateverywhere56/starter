## L’essentiel

China’s National Medical Products Administration (NMPA) has granted a Class III Medical Devices Certificate of Registration for the msRDN radiofrequency renal denervation system (SyMap Medical). The msRDN system consists of a disposable renal artery radiofrequency ablation catheter, a console with both electronic stimulation and radiofrequency ablation function.
