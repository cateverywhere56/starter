## L’essentiel

Researchers behind a new study published in the Annals of Vascular Surgery, among them lead author Laura Anderson and corresponding author Benjamin Brooke (both University of Utah Health, Salt Lake City, USA), have found that severe obesity risks a greater likelihood of maturation failure in the arteriovenous fistulas (AVFs) of patients looking to start dialysis.
