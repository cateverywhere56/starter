## L’essentiel

NeoDynamics, a medical device company dedicated to advancing the diagnosis and care of cancer, recently announced that the company has been granted a Chinese patent for the needle design employed in the NeoNavia FlexiPulse probe. The front-loaded, open-tip sampling needle is designed to enable maximum tissue yield, with minimal patient trauma.
