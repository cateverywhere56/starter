## L’essentiel

Instylla has announced the enrolment of the first patient in the HALT study for the treatment of peripheral arterial bleeds. The HALT study is a prospective, multicentre, single-arm study to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of the Embrace hydrogel embolic system (HES) for the embolization of arterial bleeding in solid organs and peripheral arteries.
