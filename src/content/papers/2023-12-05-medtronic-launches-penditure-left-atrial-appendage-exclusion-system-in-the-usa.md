## L’essentiel

Medtronic has announced the launch of its Penditure left atrial appendage (LAA) exclusion system in the USA. The Penditure LAA Exclusion System is an implantable clip that comes pre-loaded on a single-use delivery system for use in left atrial appendage management (LAAM) during concomitant cardiac surgery procedures.
