## L’essentiel

A patient in Cincinnati has become the first in the USA to receive a system that enables remote monitoring of his heart and congestion status, as well as self-adjustment of diuretics. Vectorious Medical Technologies developed a miniature pressure sensor that is implanted within the heart via standard minimally invasive transvenous catheterisation.
