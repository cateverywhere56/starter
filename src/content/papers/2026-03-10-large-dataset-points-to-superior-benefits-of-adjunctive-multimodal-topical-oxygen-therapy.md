## L’essentiel

A large retrospective review of 3,126 patients with chronic wounds treated with multimodality intermittent topical oxygen therapy (ITOT) presented at the 2026 American Venous Forum (AVF; 28 February–March 4, Denver, USA) demonstrated superior and more durable wound healing outcomes in complex, comorbid populations compared to those reported in population-based, real-world studies, according to study data.
