## L’essentiel

Randomised trials remain the cornerstone of evidence in interventional radiology (IR), yet they are resource intensive and slow to yield results. Retrospective studies provide a practical complement by leveraging existing clinical data to describe real-world practice, capture rare events and long-term outcomes, and generate hypotheses efficiently.
