## L’essentiel

In the TRANSCEND clinical trial, the SurVeil drug-coated balloon (DCB; Surmodics) demonstrated &#8220;excellent efficacy and safety&#8221; out to 24-month follow-up. This is according to Kenneth Rosenfield (Massachusetts General Hospital, Boston, USA), who presented the new data at Vascular Interventional Advances (VIVA) 2022 (31 October–3 November, Las Vegas, USA).
