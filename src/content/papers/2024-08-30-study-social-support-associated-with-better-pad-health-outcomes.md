## L’essentiel

Patients with peripheral arterial disease (PAD) reporting lower levels of social support experience worse health outcomes, a new Yale-led study finds. Social support is thought to bolster cardiovascular health by facilitating health-promoting behaviours and acting as a buffer against the impacts of stress on the heart.
