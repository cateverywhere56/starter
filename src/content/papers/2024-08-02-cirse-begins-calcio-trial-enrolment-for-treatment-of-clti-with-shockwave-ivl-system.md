## L’essentiel

The Cardiovascular and Interventional Society of Europe (CIRSE) has announced that patient enrolment for the CALCIO trial has begun. CALCIO started patient enrolment on 15 July, 2024, with the goal of recruiting 400 chronic limb-threatening ischaemia (CLTI) patients over the next two years.
