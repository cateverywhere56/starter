## L’essentiel

Gore recently announced the first implantation of the investigational Gore ascending stent graft in the ARISE III trial for the treatment of an acute type A dissection. The implant was performed at Emory University (Atlanta, USA) by Brad Leshnower, site principal investigator, and Yazan Duwayri, study investigator.
