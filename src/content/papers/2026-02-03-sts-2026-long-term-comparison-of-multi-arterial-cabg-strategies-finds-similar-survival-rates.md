## L’essentiel

Two of the most common strategies for multi-arterial coronary artery bypass graft (CABG)—using either the bilateral internal thoracic artery (BITA) or single internal thoracic artery plus radial artery (SITA+RA)—provide comparable long-term survival overall, with BITA offering a modest advantage among younger patients, compared to a small advantage for SITA+RA in older patients.
