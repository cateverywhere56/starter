## L’essentiel

The first patient has been enrolled in a UK study of large vessel de novo coronary artery disease treated with Selution SLR (MedAlliance) sirolimus-eluting balloon. This is a trial initiated by physicians at the Wrightington, Wigan and Leigh Teaching Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust, where the first patient was enrolled.
