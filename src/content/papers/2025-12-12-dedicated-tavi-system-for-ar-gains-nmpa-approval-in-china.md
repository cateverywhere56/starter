## L’essentiel

Peijia Medical has announced that its TaurusTrio transcatheter aortic valve (TAV) system received approval from the National Medical Products Administration (NMPA) of China. The TaurusTrio TAV system is developed and manufactured by Peijia Medical under an exclusive license from JenaValve Technology (JenaValve) for its Trilogy transcatheter heart valve (THV) system.
