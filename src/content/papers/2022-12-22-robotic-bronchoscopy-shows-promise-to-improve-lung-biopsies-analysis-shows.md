## L’essentiel

Lung nodule biopsies performed with new robotic bronchoscopy technology may be safer and more effective than those done by traditional methods, a study by researchers at the University of Texas Southwestern (UTSW; Dallas-Fort Worth, USA) suggests. UTSW was among the first in the country to use robotic-assisted bronchoscopy (RAB) to biopsy pulmonary lesions.
