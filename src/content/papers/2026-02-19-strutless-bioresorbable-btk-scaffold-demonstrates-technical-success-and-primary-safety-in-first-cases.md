## L’essentiel

Preliminary three-month results from the first five human implantations of StentIt’s resorbable fibrillated scaffold (RFS) reveal a technical success rate of 100% and no device- or procedure-related serious adverse events at 30 days follow-up in patients with below-the-knee (BTK) chronic limb-threatening ischaemia (CLTI).
