## L’essentiel

The Society of Interventional Oncology (SIO) has announced that the first patient has been enrolled in the TRIBUTE trial at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, USA. The multicentre observational trial will investigate symptomatic high-risk bone metastases treated with percutaneous ablation and palliative radiation therapy.
