## L’essentiel

Prevencio has been issued a patent from the United States Patent and Trademark Office for its blood test, HART CADhs, which assesses a patient’s risk of obstructive coronary artery disease. The company utilised its artificial intelligence (AI)-driven HART platform and expertise in cardiac blood tests to develop HART CADhs.
