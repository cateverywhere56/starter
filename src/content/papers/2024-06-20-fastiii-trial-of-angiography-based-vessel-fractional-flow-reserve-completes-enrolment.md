## L’essentiel

Pie Medical Imaging has announced the completion of enrolment in FASTIII, a multicentre randomised clinical trial, investigating the use of angiography-based vessel fractional flow reserve (CAAS vFFR) in patients undergoing coronary revascularisation procedures. vFFR can assess whether a coronary artery narrowing is functionally significant and may require revascularisation.
