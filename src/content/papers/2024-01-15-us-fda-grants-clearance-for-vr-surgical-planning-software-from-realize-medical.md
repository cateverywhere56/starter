## L’essentiel

Realize Medical has announced that its virtual reality (VR) software for surgical planning, Elucis, has received 510(k) clearance from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Elucis enables medical professionals to quickly create, understand, and share complex patient anatomy from computed tomography (CT) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) in an immersive 3D environment.
