## L’essentiel

Robocath has announced a first-in-human robotic coronary angioplasty performed at a distance of 1,700 miles spanning two Chinese institutions. Two interventional cardiologists, Yundai Chen in Beijing and Yining Yang in Urumqi, the capital of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, performed the procedure.
