## L’essentiel

Merit Medical Systems has announced that it has completed the acquisition of a portfolio of dialysis catheter products and the BioSentry biopsy tract sealant system. “We are selectively investing to expand our product portfolio in key strategic markets that leverage our existing commercial footprint,” said Fred P Lampropoulos, Merit’s chairman and CEO.
