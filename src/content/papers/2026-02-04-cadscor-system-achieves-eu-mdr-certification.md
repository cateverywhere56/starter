## L’essentiel

Acarix has announced that its CADScor system has successfully achieved certification under the European Union Medical Device Regulation (EU MDR 2017/745). The MDR certification confirms that the CADScor system meets the EU’s most stringent regulatory requirements for safety, quality, and clinical performance.
