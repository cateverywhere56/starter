## L’essentiel

Abbott has received CE mark in Europe for the Aveir dual chamber (DR) leadless pacemaker system. The Aveir DR leadless pacemaker system utilises a new method of delivering dual chamber therapy as it is comprised of two unique devices—one that paces the right ventricle (Aveir VR) and one that paces the right atrium (Aveir AR).
