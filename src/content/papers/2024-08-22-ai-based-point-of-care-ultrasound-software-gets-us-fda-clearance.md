## L’essentiel

AISAP has announced that the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has granted 510(k) clearance for its AI-powered AISAP CARDIO point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS) software platform. The cloud-based platform combines four computer-assisted diagnosis (CADx) modules of valvular pathologies and eight key measurements into a single software package that automatically generates analyses, interpretations and reports.
