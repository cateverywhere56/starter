## L’essentiel

Magenta Medical, developer of the Elevate heart pump, has closed a US$105 million financing round led by global healthcare investment firm Novo Holdings. New investors Viking Global Investors and RA Capital Management, and existing investors OrbiMed, New Enterprise Associates (NEA), JVC Investment Partners, and ALIVE &#8211; Israel HealthTech Fund, also participated in this round.
