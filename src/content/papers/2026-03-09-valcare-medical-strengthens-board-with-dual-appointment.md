## L’essentiel

Valcare Medical has announced the appointment of Jeffrey B Jump and Jacques Séguin to its board of directors. The appointments will provide strategic guidance as Valcare advances enrolment in the AMEND TS early feasibility study (EFS) of the Amend transcatheter mitral annuloplasty system and prepares for subsequent clinical trials.
