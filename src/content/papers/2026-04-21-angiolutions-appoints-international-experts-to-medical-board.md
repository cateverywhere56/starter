## L’essentiel

Angiolutions has announced the appointment of four internationally recognised vascular and endovascular specialists to its medical board. The new board will support the company as it advances development of its investigational A3-Shield system, a first-in-class implant designed to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAAs) by locally modulating aortic pulse waves.
