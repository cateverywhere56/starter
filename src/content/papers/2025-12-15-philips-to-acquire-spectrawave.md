## L’essentiel

Royal Philips has entered into an agreement to acquire SpectraWAVE, an innovator in Enhanced Vascular Imaging (EVI) of coronary arteries, angiography-based physiology assessments, and the use of artificial intelligence (AI) in medical imaging. SpectraWAVE, based in Bedford, USA, was founded in 2017 and currently employs more than 70 people.
