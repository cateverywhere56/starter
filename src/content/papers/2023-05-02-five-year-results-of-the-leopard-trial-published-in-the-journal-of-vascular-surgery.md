## L’essentiel

Endologix has announced the online publication of the final five-year results of the LEOPARD trial in the Journal of Vascular Surgery (JVS). The study&#8217;s findings showed that there was no significant difference in aneurysm-related outcomes between patients randomised to the AFX endograft system, with anatomical fixation, and commercially available endografts with proximal fixation.
