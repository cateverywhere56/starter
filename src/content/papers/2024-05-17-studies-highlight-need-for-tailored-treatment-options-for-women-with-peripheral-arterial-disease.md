## L’essentiel

New clinical results highlight the need for inclusive approaches and comprehensive examinations of treatment options for peripheral arterial disease (PAD), including endovascular therapy and revascularisation. The data were presented recently at the Society for Cardiovascular Angiography &#38; Interventions (SCAI) 2024 Scientific Sessions (2–4 May, Long Beach, USA).
