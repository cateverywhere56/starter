## L’essentiel

Surgeons at the University of Maryland Medical Center (UMMC, Baltimore, USA) have performed the second transplant using a genetically modified pig heart into a patient deemed ineligible for heart transplant. The procedure, involving a 58-year-old patient with terminal heart disease took place on 20 September.
