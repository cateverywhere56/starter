## L’essentiel

Elumn8 Medical—formerly known as Simpson Interventions—has announced the successful initial close of its series C financing, coinciding with the company’s rebrand. The financing will support continued clinical development of the Acolyte image-guided crossing and re-entry catheter system, currently being evaluated in a premarket clinical trial for the treatment of coronary chronic total occlusions (CTOs).
