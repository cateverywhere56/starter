## L’essentiel

Central venous catheters (CVCs) are a widely used form of dialysis access, yet for years the clinical community has avoided discussing how to improve them. That silence ended at the 2026 Charing Cross (CX) Symposium (21–23 April, London, UK), where multiple presentations placed CVCs firmly in the spotlight.
