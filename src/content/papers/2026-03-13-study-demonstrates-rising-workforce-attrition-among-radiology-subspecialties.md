## L’essentiel

A new retrospective study from the Harvey L Neiman Health Policy Institute in Reston, USA, has highlighted rising attrition rates across radiology, to a varying degree among subspecialties. The analysis, published in the American Journal of Roentgenology, was based on Medicare-enrolled radiologists from 2014–2022.
