## L’essentiel

Robocath has announced the launch of a first-in-human (FIH) clinical study evaluating its new robotic system in coronary artery disease. The study will recruit 20 patients with coronary artery disease, primarily complex cases representative of the routine activity of a catheterisation laboratory.
