## L’essentiel

JenaValve Technology has announced that it will join the American Society of Echocardiography (ASE) Industry Roundtable in 2024. In a press release, JenaValve said that the organisations will work together to drive increased awareness and education of cardiac imaging for the detection and diagnosis of aortic regurgitation (AR).
