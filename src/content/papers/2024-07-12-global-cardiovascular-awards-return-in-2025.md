## L’essentiel

Entries have opened for the second edition of the Global Cardiovascular Awards, celebrating innovation and excellence in the field of cardiovascular healthcare. Returning on 13 March 2025, the second ever Global Cardiovascular Awards will feature 21 award categories recognising achievements in cardiovascular patient care, research innovation and innovation, with 11 categories added for 2025.
