## L’essentiel

The DynamX (Elixir Medical) bioadaptor system met non-inferiority at one year for target lesion failure (TLF) in a trial comparing the device against the Resolute Onyx (Medtronic) drug-eluting stent (DES) in a broad population of patients undergoing percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI).
