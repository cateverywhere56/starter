## L’essentiel

Endologix recently announced the completion of the 150th case in the JAGUAR study. This randomised controlled trial is evaluating Endologix’s Alto abdominal stent graft system, comparing its effectiveness to other commercially available endovascular aneurysm repair (EVAR) devices in the treatment of abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAA).
