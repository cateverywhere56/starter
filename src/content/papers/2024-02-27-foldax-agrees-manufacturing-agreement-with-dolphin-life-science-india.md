## L’essentiel

Foldax has announced a manufacturing agreement with Dolphin Life Science India to enable in-country manufacturing of the Tria polymer mitral surgical heart valve outside of the USA for the first time. The Tria mitral valve is designed specifically to accommodate the anatomy and pressures of the mitral position.
