## L’essentiel

Surmodics recently announced it has received a letter from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) related to its premarket approval (PMA) application for the SurVeil drug-coated balloon (DCB). In the letter, the FDA indicated that the application is not currently approvable, while providing specific guidance as to a path forward.
