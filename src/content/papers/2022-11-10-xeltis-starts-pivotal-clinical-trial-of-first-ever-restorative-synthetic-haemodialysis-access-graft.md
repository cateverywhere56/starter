## L’essentiel

Xeltis announced today the initiation of a pivotal trial with the “first-ever” restorative synthetic haemodialysis access grafts, dubbed ‘Axess’. The first two patients have been successfully implanted as part of the AXESS European pivotal trial at AZ Sint-Jan Brugge-Oostende AV (Bruges, Belgium) by vascular surgeon Jan De Letter, and discharged from hospital.
