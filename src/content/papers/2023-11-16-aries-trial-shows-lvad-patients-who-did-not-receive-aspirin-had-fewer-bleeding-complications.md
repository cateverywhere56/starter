## L’essentiel

Abbott has announced new late-breaking data that show advanced heart failure patients living with its HeartMate 3 left ventricular assist device (LVAD) who did not receive aspirin experienced fewer complications from bleeding and were associated with reduced hospital visits compared to patients who took aspirin daily following their implant.
