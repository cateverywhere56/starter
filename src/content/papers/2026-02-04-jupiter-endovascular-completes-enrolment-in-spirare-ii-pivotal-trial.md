## L’essentiel

Jupiter Endovascular today announced completion of patient enrolment in the SPIRARE II pivotal clinical trial evaluating the Vertex pulmonary embolectomy system in patients with acute, intermediate-risk pulmonary embolism (PE). The final subject was enrolled by Vikas Aggarwal at Henry Ford Hospital in Detroit, USA—the site’s principal investigator.
