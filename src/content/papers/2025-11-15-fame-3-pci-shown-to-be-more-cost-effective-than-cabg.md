## L’essentiel

Economic analysis of the FAME 3 trial, comparing fractional flow reserve (FFR)-guided percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI) to coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, has shown that CABG incurred higher costs over the five-year lifespan of the trial.
