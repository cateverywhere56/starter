## L’essentiel

Emboline has closed US$20 Million in growth capital, supporting its efforts to advance new technology for reducing stroke and ischaemic damage during structural heart procedures. Emboline recently completed enrolment in the PROTECT H2H investigational device exemption (IDE) clinical trial, a prospective randomised study evaluating the Emboliner embolic protection system.
