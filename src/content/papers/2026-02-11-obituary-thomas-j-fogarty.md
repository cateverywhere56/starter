## L’essentiel

Thomas J Fogarty, the world-renowned cardiovascular surgeon, innovator and inventor of the Fogarty catheter, died peacefully on 28 December 2025 at the age of 91. Colleagues at Fogarty Innovation, a nonprofit educational incubator dedicated to advancing medical innovation, announced the news of their founder’s passing.
