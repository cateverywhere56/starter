## L’essentiel

University of Leicester experts have been awarded nearly £2 million to set up a global vascular research centre, based at the National Institute for Health and Care Research (NIHR) Leicester Biomedical Research Centre, a press release reports. The funding, from the NIHR Research Professorships scheme, will support the creation of a new programme called CIRCULIFE.
