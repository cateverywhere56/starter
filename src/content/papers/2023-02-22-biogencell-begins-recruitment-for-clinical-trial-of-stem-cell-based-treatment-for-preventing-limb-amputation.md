## L’essentiel

BioGenCell has announced that it is recruiting for an international clinical trial to assess the company&#8217;s stem cell-based therapy for chronic limb-threatening ischaemia patients who are at risk for limb amputation. The clinical trial is currently taking place in medical centres in Israel, Europe, and the USA, a press release details.
