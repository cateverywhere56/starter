## L’essentiel

Reflow Medical recently announced it has received CE mark certification in the European Union for the Bare Temporary Spur stent system. The device is intended to treat de novo or restenotic lesions in the infrapopliteal arteries with a commercially available drug-coated balloon (DCB) to enhance drug absorption.
