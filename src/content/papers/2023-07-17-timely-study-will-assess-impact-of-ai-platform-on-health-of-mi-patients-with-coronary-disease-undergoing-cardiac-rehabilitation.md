## L’essentiel

The TIMELY consortium announced today the enrolment of the first patient in the randomised controlled clinical TIMELY trial. The study investigates to what extent the artificial intelligence (AI)-supported TIMELY platform improves the health of myocardial infarction (MI) patients and other patients with coronary heart disease after cardiac rehabilitation.
