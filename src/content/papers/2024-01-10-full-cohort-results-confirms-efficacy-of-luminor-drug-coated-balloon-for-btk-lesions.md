## L’essentiel

iVascular reflect on the Luminor drug-coated balloon (DCB) in below-the-knee (BTK) outcomes obtained in the BIBLIOS trial one-year follow-up presented at the Paris Vascular Insights (PVI) 2023 (8–10 November, Paris, France). BIBLIOS is a prospective, single-arm, multicentre study conducted in eight sites across Belgium, Italy and Spain.
