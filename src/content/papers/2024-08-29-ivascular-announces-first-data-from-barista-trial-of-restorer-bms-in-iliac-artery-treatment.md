## L’essentiel

iVascular has announced the first clinical outcomes of the Restorer bare metal stent (BMS) in iliac artery treatment. The data were presented earlier this summer at the Leipzig Interventional Course (LINC 2024; 28–31 May, Leipzig, Germany) by Lieven Maene (OLV Hospital, Aalst, Belgium), principal investigator of the study.
