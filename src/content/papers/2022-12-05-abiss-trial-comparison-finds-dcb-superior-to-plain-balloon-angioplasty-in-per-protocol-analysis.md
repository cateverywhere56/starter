## L’essentiel

Data have been presented for the first time at the Paris Vascular Insights (PVI) conference (23–25 November, Paris, France) from the ABISS trial, which compared drug-coated balloon (DCB) with plain-balloon angioplasty for arteriovenous fistula (AVF) stenosis. ABISS was a physician-designed prospective, randomised, double-blind trial that included 12 centres in France.
