## L’essentiel

Cardiovascular Systems (CSI) announced that Innova Vascular (Innova) has submitted a 510(k) premarket notification to the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for its thrombectomy devices intended to treat peripheral vascular disease. CSI intends to acquire and commercialise each of the novel thrombectomy devices from Innova targeting peripheral vascular disease.
