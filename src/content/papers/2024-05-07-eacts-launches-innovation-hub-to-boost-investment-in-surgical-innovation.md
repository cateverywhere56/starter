## L’essentiel

The European Association for Cardio-Thoracic Surgery (EACTS) has launched a new Innovation Hub, which will champion investment in innovation to support the development of cardiothoracic surgical treatments and improve outcomes for patients. The Innovation Hub was announced at the Association’s annual Innovation Summit (19–20 April, Paris, France).
