## L’essentiel

Results from the PROMISE II pivotal trial investigating transcatheter arterialisation of the deep veins using the LimFlow system in so-called no-option chronic limb-threatening ischemia (CLTI) patients were today published in the New England Journal of Medicine (NEJM).
