## L’essentiel

Results of a sub-study from the pivotal investigational device exemption (IDE) trial assessing Gore’s Excluder conformable abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA) endoprosthesis have shown that the device is safe and effective in hostile angulated neck anatomy, with the results maintained out to one-year.
