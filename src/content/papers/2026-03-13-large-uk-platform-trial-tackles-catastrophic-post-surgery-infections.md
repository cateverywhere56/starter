## L’essentiel

ROSSINI-Platform—said to be the largest-ever UK surgical trial—is set to commence in the coming months with the aim of assessing various methods for reducing post-intervention surgical site infection (SSI). The project will encompass six parallel randomised controlled trials (RCTs) across several surgical specialties, including vascular surgery.
