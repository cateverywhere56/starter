## L’essentiel

Philips has announced the expanded commercial availability of LumiGuide 3D device guidance—the much-heralded first real-time artificial intelligence (AI)-enabled light-based navigation solution for image-guided therapy—across Europe and the USA. LumiGuide integrates with Philips&#8217; Azurion platform, which is designed to drive procedural innovation across clinical domains.
