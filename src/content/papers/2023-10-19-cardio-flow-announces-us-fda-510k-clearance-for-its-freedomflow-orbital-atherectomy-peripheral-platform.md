## L’essentiel

Cardio Flow recently announced it has received US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) 510(k) clearance for the company’s FreedomFlow orbital atherectomy peripheral platform. The company details in a press release that the FreedomFlow platform is designed with a modern mechanism of action to clear plaque blockages in the arteries of the legs.
