## L’essentiel

The American College of Cardiology (ACC) has named the influential interventional cardiologist and clinical trialist Roxana Mehran (Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, New York, USA) as its new president. Her appointment was confirmed during the convocation ceremony at the American College of Cardiology’s annual scientific session (ACC.26; 28–30 March, New Orleans, USA).
