## L’essentiel

Haemonetics Corporation today announced the acquisition of Vivasure Medical, a Galway, Ireland-based company developing next-generation technology for percutaneous vessel closure. Vivasure&#8217;s PerQseal Elite system uses a proprietary bioabsorbable patch to seal large-bore (up to 26Fr) arteriotomies and venotomies from inside the vessel, offering a sutureless, fully absorbable solution for structural heart and endovascular procedures.
