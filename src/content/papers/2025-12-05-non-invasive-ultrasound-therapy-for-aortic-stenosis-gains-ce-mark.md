## L’essentiel

Cardiawave has received CE certification for Valvosoft, its non-invasive therapeutic alternative to treat severe symptomatic aortic stenosis. Valvosoft is designed to non-invasively restore leaflet mobility in a stenotic aortic valve and widen the valve opening to relieve patient heart failure symptoms.
