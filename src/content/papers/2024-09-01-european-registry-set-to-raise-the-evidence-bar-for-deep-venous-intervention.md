## L’essentiel

The pilot phase of a new European Venous Registry (EVeR)—designed to illuminate the real-world outcomes of deep venous interventions over a 10-year time horizon—is set to launch this month (September 2024). Developed and hosted by the European Society for Vascular Surgery (ESVS), the registry will be an international repository of deep venous treatment data.
