## L’essentiel

Surmodics has announced successful early clinical use of the company’s Pounce LP (low-profile) thrombectomy system. The Pounce LP system, which received US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) clearance in June 2023, is currently in limited market evaluation (LME), with full commercial launch planned following completion of the LME.
