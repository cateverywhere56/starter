## L’essentiel

Surmodics announced in a press release that it has received US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) 510(k) clearance for its Pounce LP (low profile) thrombectomy system. Introduced in 2021, the Pounce thrombectomy system is intended for the non-surgical removal of thrombi and emboli from the peripheral arterial vasculature in vessels 3.5mm to 6mm in diameter.
