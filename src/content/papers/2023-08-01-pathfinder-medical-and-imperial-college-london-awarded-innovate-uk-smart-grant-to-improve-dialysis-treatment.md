## L’essentiel

Pathfinder Medical has been awarded a Smart grant for a £1.1 million (US$1.4 million) project by Innovate UK, the UK&#8217;s innovation agency. A press release details that the grant will support Pathfinder’s novel project to develop a minimally invasive arteriovenous fistula (AVF) at the wrist for patients with renal failure who require dialysis.
