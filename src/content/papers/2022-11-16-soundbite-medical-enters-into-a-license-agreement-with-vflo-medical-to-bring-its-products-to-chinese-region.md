## L’essentiel

Soundbite Medical Solutions announced that it has entered into an exclusive license agreement with VFLO Medical (VFLO). Pursuant to the agreement, Soundbite has granted VFLO an exclusive license for certain proprietary products including its SoundBite crossing system and the Active microcatheter system.
