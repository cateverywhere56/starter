## L’essentiel

Adept Medical has launched the Adducted Arm Scoop—an improved solution for supporting the adducted arms of supine patients during image-guided procedures. Adept’s new, “extremely durable” Adducted Arm Scoops are purpose-designed, providing security and comfort on narrow imaging tables, as per a press release.
