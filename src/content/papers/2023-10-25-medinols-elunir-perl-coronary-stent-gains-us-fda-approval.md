## L’essentiel

Medinol today announced US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) approval for the EluNIR-PERL drug-eluting stent (DES) for the treatment of coronary artery disease. EluNIR-PERL builds upon the performance and clinical data of the EluNIR DES system.
