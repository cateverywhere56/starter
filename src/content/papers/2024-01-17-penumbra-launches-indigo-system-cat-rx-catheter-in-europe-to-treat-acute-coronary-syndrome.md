## L’essentiel

Penumbra has secured CE mark for its Indigo system CAT RX designed to address the limitations of traditional management strategies in acute coronary syndrome (ACS), the company has announced. Traditional modalities utilising syringe aspiration suffer from diminished vacuum once fluid enters the system, the company says in a press release.
