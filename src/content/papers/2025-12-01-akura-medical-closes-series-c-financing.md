## L’essentiel

Akura Medical has secured a US$53 million first close in series C financing, with the funds to be used to support development activities for the Katana thrombectomy system and the NavIQ quantification software, alongside completion of enrolment in the QUADRA-PE clinical trial and regulatory submissions.
