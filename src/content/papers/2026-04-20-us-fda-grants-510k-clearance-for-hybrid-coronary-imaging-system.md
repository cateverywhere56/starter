## L’essentiel

Conavi Medical has announced that it has received US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) 510(k) clearance for its next-generation hybrid coronary imaging system. The milestone positions the company to initiate its US commercial launch expected in the second half of 2026 and to expand its hybrid intravascular imaging technology.
