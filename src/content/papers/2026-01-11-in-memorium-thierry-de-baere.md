## L’essentiel

Thierry de Baère, a pioneering figure in interventional oncology (IO) specialising in intra-arterial and locoregional therapies, has died. Born in paris in1962, De Baère studied at the University of Paris-Sud and completed his residency at the Université de Caen Basse in Normandy, France.
