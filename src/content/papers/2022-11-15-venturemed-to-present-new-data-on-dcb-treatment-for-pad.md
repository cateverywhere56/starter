## L’essentiel

VentureMed Group has announced new data presentations on the treatment of peripheral arterial disease (PAD) with drug-coated balloon (DCBs) in addition to 12-month AV Registry Data. These presentations will be given as part of the VEITH Symposium Annual Scientific Meeting 2022 (15–19 November, New York, USA).
