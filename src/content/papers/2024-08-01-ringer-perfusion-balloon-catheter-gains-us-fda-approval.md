## L’essentiel

Teleflex has announced US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) 510(k) clearance of the Ringer perfusion balloon catheter (PBC), describing it as the only commercially available percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA) perfusion balloon. The Ringer PBC is a rapid-exchange 0.014” compatible catheter with a helical balloon at its working end.
