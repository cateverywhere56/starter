## L’essentiel

Transit Scientific announces the successful clinical deployment of XO Cath, an embolic delivery microcatheter intended for interventional procedures. Richard Saxon (North County Radiology Associates, San Diego, USA), performed the inaugural procedure utilising the XO Cath 2.0Fr 130cm microcatheter with a Bern-shaped tip.
