## L’essentiel

Stent implantation may not be beneficial in many patients with spontaneous coronary artery dissection (SCAD), research presented at the European Association of Percutaneous Cardiovascular Interventions (EAPCI) Summit (19–20 February, Munich, Germany) indicates. SCAD is an emergency condition in which an inner layer of a coronary artery tears away from the outer layer.
