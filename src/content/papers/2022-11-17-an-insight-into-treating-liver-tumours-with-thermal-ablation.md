## L’essentiel

Amanda Smolock (Medical College of Wisconsin, Milwaukee, USA) discusses how her practice operates, using microwave ablation nearly exclusively for primary and metastatic liver tumours targetable under ultrasound (US) and/or computed tomography (CT) guidance. Patients are typically discussed at a multidisciplinary liver tumour conference and referred to the interventional radiology (IR) clinic for evaluation.
