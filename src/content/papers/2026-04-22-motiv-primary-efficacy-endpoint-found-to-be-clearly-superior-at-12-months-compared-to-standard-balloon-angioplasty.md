## L’essentiel

Tim Wittig (Leipzig, Germany) has shared primary outcomes from MOTIV-BTK as a podium-first presentation at the 2026 Charing Cross (CX) Symposium (21–23 April, London, UK). This prospective, multicentre, single-blind randomised controlled trial is designed to assess the Motiv sirolimus-eluting bioresorbable scaffold (Reva Medical).
