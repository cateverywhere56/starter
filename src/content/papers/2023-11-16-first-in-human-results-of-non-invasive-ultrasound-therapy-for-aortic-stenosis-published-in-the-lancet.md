## L’essentiel

Results from first-in-human (FIH) clinical studies on 40 patients suffering from calcific aortic stenosis treated with Cardiawave’s non-invasive ultrasound therapy (NIUT) have been published in The Lancet. The clinical studies series, enrolled 40 patients with severe symptomatic aortic valve stenosis at three clinical sites in France, The Netherlands and in Serbia.
