## L’essentiel

Biotronik has launched the Micro Rx novel rapid exchange microcatheter to enhance guidewire support during percutaneous coronary interventions (PCI) in the USA. The device, distributed by Biotronik and manufactured by Interventional Medical Device Solutions (IMDS), is the fourth IMDS product Biotronik has brought to the USA, including the NHancer Rx, TrapIT, and ReCross catheters.
