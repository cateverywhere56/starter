## L’essentiel

Starlight Cardiovascular has announced that the first baby in the world was treated with its Lifeline Ductus Arteriosus stent system at Cincinnati Children&#8217;s Hospital (Cincinnati, USA) in January. The device is currently being studied under a US Food and Drug Administration Investigational Device Exemption clinical trial.
