## L’essentiel

Preliminary results from the largest global liver venous deprivation (LVD) registry—EuroLVD—confirms &#8220;high&#8221; hypertrophy and kinetic growth rate and &#8220;very low&#8221; rate of post-hepatectomy liver failure (PHLF), demonstrating the &#8220;safety&#8221; of LVD over portal vein embolization (PVE) in a real-world, multicentre setting.
