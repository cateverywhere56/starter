## L’essentiel

Cardiothoracic surgeon Gilbert Tang (Mount Sinai Health System, New York, USA) has been named editor-in-chief of JACC: Case Reports, assuming the role from August 2024. “I am both honoured and humbled to be a cardiac surgeon among the editor-in-chiefs in the JACC family of journals,” Tang said.
