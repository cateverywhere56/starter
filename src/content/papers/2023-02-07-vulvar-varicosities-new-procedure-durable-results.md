## L’essentiel

In the past two decades, there has been a shift in varicose vein management from conventional surgical treatments to outpatient interventional procedures. Depending on the type and site of the affected vein, patients now have many other treatment options—namely with ablation, glue, coil embolization and sclerotherapy.
