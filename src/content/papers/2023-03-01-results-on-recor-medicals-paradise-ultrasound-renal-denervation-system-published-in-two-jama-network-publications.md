## L’essentiel

ReCor Medical and its parent company, Otsuka Medical Devices, recently announced that primary endpoint results from the RADIANCE II pivotal trial were published in the Journal of the American Medical Association (JAMA). Study results showed that the Paradise ultrasound renal denervation (uRDN) system successfully reduced blood pressure compared to sham.
