## L’essentiel

Half Moon Medical has announced the treatment of 15 patients with its second generation Half Moon transcatheter mitral valve repair (TMVR) technology as part of an ongoing pilot study. The investigational Half Moon mitral valve repair device is designed to restore mitral valve function in patients with severe symptomatic mitral regurgitation (MR).
