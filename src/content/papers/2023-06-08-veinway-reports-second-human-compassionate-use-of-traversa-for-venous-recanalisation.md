## L’essentiel

In a second compassionate use case, interventional vascular surgeon Stefan Stalhoff of Klinikum Hochsauerland (Arnsberg, Germany) used VeinWay&#8216;s Traversa for venous recanalisation to save a patient&#8217;s arm, with supervision by VeinWay scientific advisory board member Michael Lichtenberg. The patient had been in so much pain, that she could not hold her young children.
