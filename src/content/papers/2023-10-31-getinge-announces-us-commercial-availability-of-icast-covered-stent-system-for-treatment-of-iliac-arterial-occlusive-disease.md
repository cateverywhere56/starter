## L’essentiel

Getinge has announced commercial availability of the iCast covered stent system in the USA for the treatment of iliac arterial occlusive disease. In March 2023, the iCast covered stent received premarket approval from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for the treatment of iliac arterial occlusive disease.
