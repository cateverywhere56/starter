## L’essentiel

FlowPhysix, formerly known as Expanse ICE, has announced a strategic partnership with 3comma Medical who will serve as their international commercial partner for their FlowRunner aspiration thrombectomy system. This partnership between FlowPhysix and 3comma will provide physicians outside of the USA access to new standards in thrombectomy technology, enabling FlowPhysix to focus on US commercialisation.
