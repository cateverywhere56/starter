## L’essentiel

On 24 May, the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) granted an investigational device exemption (IDE) approval for Concept Medical&#8217;s MagicTouch percutaneous transluminal angioplasty (PTA) sirolimus-coated balloon (SCB) for treatment of the superficial femoral artery (SFA). This marks the fourth IDE approval for the company&#8217;s SCB, a press release reports.
