## L’essentiel

Gore announced today the initiation of the Gore VBX FORWARD clinical study to compare the VBX stent graft to bare metal stenting for patients with complex iliac occlusive disease. This prospective, multicentre, randomised controlled trial will include up to 40 sites in the USA, Australia, New sealand and Europe.
