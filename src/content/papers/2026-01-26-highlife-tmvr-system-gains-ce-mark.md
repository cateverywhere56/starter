## L’essentiel

HighLife SAS has announced that it has received CE mark approval for the HighLife transcatheter mitral valve replacement (TMVR) system for the treatment of adult patients suffering from symptomatic moderate-severe or severe mitral regurgitation (MR), who are deemed unsuitable for surgical repair or replacement and transcatheter edge-to-edge repair (TEER) by a multidisciplinary heart team.
