## L’essentiel

Patients who received warfarin after bioprosthetic aortic valve replacement had lower incidence of mortality and a decreased risk of blood clots, according to a retrospective study published in Mayo Clinic Proceedings. Research has been conflicting on whether patients would benefit from more aggressive early postoperative anticoagulation treatment, the researchers claim.
