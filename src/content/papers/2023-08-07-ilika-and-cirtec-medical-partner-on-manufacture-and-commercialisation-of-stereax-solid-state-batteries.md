## L’essentiel

Ilika and Cirtec Medical have announced that they have signed a 10-year manufacturing licence to produce the Stereax range of mm-scale batteries at Cirtec’s facility in Lowell, USA. Ilika, which is based in Romsey, UK, develops solid-state batteries, which can be used in a range of applications including cardiac sensing, neurostimulation and smart orthopaedics.
