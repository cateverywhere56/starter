## L’essentiel

Endotronix has submitted a premarket approval (PMA) application for its Cordella pulmonary artery (PA) sensor system to the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Cordella is a heart failure patient management platform that delivers proactive PA pressure data and non-invasive vital health data for comprehensive heart failure management at home.
