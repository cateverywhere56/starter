## L’essentiel

CathWorks has announced that the CathWorks FFRangio system has been approved under the European Union (EU) Medical Device Regulation, commonly referred to as EU MDR, and fulfils the requirements for CE marking. EU MDR, the new medical device regulation applicable in Europe, replaces the previous Medical Device Directive.
