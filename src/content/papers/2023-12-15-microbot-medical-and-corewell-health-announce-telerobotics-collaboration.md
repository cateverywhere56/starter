## L’essentiel

Microbot Medical, developer of the Liberty endovascular robotic surgical system, has entered into a collaboration agreement with Corewell Health. The objective of the collaboration, which will take place in multiple phases, is to enable telerobotics between remote centres by utilising the Liberty robotic system.
