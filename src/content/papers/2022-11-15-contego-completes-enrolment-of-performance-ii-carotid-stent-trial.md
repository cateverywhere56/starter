## L’essentiel

Contego Medical has announced that enrolment of the PERFORMANCE II clinical trial has been completed. PERFORMANCE II is intended to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of the Neuroguard integrated embolic protection (IEP) three-in-one carotid stent and post-dilation balloon system with IEP.
