## L’essentiel

Rijnstate Hospital in Arnhem, The Netherlands, recently announced that the first patient has been enrolled in the HERCULES trial comparing endosuture aneurysm repair (ESAR) to standard endovascular aneurysm repair (EVAR) in the treatment of abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAAs) with wide proximal necks.
