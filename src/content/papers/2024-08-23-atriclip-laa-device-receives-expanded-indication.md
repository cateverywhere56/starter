## L’essentiel

AtriCure has received an expanded indication for the AtriClip left atrial appendage (LAA) exclusion system in CE-marked countries in Europe. The product is now indicated for use in patients at high risk of thromboembolism for whom left atrial appendage exclusion is warranted.
