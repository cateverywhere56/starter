## L’essentiel

HistoSonics has announced the first kidney patient has been treated using its histotripsy platform. The procedure was performed at the Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust (Leeds, UK) by Tze Min Wah, senior consultant radiologist and clinical lead for interventional oncology programme.
