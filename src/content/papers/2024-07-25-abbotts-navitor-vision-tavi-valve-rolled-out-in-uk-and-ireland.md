## L’essentiel

Abbott is rolling out the latest iteration of its Navitor transcatheter aortic valve implantation (TAVI) system, the Navitor Vision, across UK and Ireland. The Navitor Vision valve is a minimally invasive device that allows doctors to treat patients with severe aortic stenosis who are at high or extreme surgical risk for surgery.
