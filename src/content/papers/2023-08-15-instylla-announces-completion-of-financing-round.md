## L’essentiel

Instylla, a company developing next-generation liquid embolics for peripheral vascular embolotherapy, today announced the closing of a US$30 million financing round. This financing round was led by new investor, Delos Capital, with continued participation from the investor syndicate of Excelestar Ventures, Ascension Ventures, Catalyst Health Ventures, Amed Ventures, Sparta Group, and Incept.
