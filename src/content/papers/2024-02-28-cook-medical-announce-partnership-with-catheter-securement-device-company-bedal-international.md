## L’essentiel

Cook Medical, a global medical device manufacturer, has partnered with Bedal International, a company specialising in catheter securement devices marketed under the brand FlexGRIP. FlexGRIP devices are now available as an addition to Cook&#8217;s portfolio of percutaneous drainage products to improve patient comfort and experience during treatment.
