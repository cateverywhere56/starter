## L’essentiel

WL Gore &#38; Associates—Gore—has announced that it has entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Conformal Medical, developer of the CLAAS AcuFORM system, left atrial appendage occlusion (LAAO) technology. The transaction is expected to close in early 2026, subject to regulatory approval.
