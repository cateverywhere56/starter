## L’essentiel

YorLabs has announced the successful completion of the first clinical procedure using its US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) 510(k)-cleared intracardiac imaging system. The procedure marks the first real-world clinical use of YorLabs’ Zero-CapEx imaging platform following US FDA clearance.
