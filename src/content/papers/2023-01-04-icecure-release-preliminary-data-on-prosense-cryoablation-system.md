## L’essentiel

IceCure Medical, developer of the minimally-invasive ProSense System for cryoablation, which destroys tumours by freezing, today announced interim results from the ICESECRET study for the treatment of patients with small renal masses (SRM) who cannot be offered kidney-preserving surgery. Data were presented at the Urological Association Conference (14 December, Eilat, Israel).
