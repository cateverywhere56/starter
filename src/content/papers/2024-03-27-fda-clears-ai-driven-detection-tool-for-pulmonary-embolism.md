## L’essentiel

Medical imaging AI company Avicenna.AI has announced 510(k) clearance from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for its CINA-iPE artificial intelligence (AI)-powered tool that detects incidental pulmonary embolism (PE) during routine computed tomography (CT) scans. The CINA-iPE algorithm identifies lung blood clots detected during routine CT scans for entirely different health conditions.
