## L’essentiel

Vena Medical recently announced it has received regulatory clearance from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for the use of its flagship technology, the Vena MicroAngioscope system, in the peripheral vasculature. According to the company, the Vena MicroAngioscope system is the world’s smallest camera for a live, full-colour view from inside the vasculature.
