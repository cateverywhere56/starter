## L’essentiel

Dual antiplatelet therapy (DAPT) has demonstrated improved clinical outcomes, including reduced in-hospital stroke and mortality risks, compared to other medication regimens in carotid artery disease patients who undergo stent placement via a transcarotid artery revascularisation (TCAR) procedure.
