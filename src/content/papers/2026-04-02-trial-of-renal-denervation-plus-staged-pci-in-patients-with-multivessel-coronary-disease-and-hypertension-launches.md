## L’essentiel

Medtronic has announced the launch of the EMBRACE trial, a randomised, multicentre trial evaluating its Symplicity Spyral renal denervation system added to staged percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI) versus staged PCI alone in patients with uncontrolled hypertension and multivessel coronary artery disease.
