## L’essentiel

New guidelines from the European Association for Cardio-Thoracic Surgery (EACTS) and the Society of Thoracic Surgeons (STS) have, for the first time, recognised the aorta as “an organ in its own right”. “Recognising the aorta as an organ puts it on a par with the heart, lungs or brain.
