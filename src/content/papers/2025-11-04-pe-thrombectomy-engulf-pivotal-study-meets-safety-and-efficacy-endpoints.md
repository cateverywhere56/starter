## L’essentiel

Full results from the ENGULF pivotal study of the Hēlo thrombectomy system (Endovascular Engineering) in acute intermediate risk pulmonary embolism (PE) patients demonstrated a 0.95% major adverse event rate (MAE) at 48 hours and 25.1% reduction in right-to-left ventricular (RV/LV) ratio, meeting both its primary safety and efficacy endpoints &#8220;by a wide margin&#8221;, investigators reveal.
