## L’essentiel

Medtronic has completed a US$90 million investment in Anteris Technologies, closed immediately following Anteris’ public offering of common stock. Anteris has said that the funds raised will support execution of its global pivotal PARADIGM trial and advance global commercialisation of the DurAVR transcatheter heart valve (THV).
