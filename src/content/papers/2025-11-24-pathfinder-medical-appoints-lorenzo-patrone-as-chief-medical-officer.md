## L’essentiel

Pathfinder Medical recently announced the appointment of interventional radiologist Lorenzo Patrone (Azienda USL Toscana Centro, Florence, Italy) as its chief medical officer (CMO). Patrone has been collaborating with Pathfinder since 2019 as lead clinical consultant.
