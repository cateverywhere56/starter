## L’essentiel

Inari Medical today announced the launch of two new purpose-built products, the RevCore thrombectomy catheter, and the Triever16 Curve catheter. According to a company press release, RevCore is the first mechanical thrombectomy device designed to address venous in-stent thrombosis, an increasingly common occurrence impacting more patients every year.
