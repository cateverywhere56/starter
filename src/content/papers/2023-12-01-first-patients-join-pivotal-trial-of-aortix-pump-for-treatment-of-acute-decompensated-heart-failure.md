## L’essentiel

Procyrion has announced the enrolment of the first patients in the company’s investigational device exemption (IDE) pivotal trial. The DRAIN-HF study will evaluate the Aortix percutaneous mechanical circulatory support (pMCS) technology in patients with acute decompensated heart failure (ADHF) who are unresponsive to standard medical therapy.
