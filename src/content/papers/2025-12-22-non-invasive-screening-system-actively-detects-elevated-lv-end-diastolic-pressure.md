## L’essentiel

Ventric Health has announced publication of outcomes from the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) validation study demonstrating the accuracy of the novel Vivio system in non-invasively detecting heart failure using left ventricular end-diastolic pressure (LV-EDP). The manuscript was published online in the Journal of the American Heart Association (JAHA).
