## L’essentiel

Results from a preliminary study have demonstrated that nearly one in 10 hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) lesions were not detected on cross-sectional imaging using computed tomography (CT) or magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) when compared to lipiodol staining.
