## L’essentiel

Stryker revealed this week that it has signed a definitive agreement to acquire Amplitude Vascular Systems (AVS), which is developing a next-generation intravascular lithotripsy (IVL) platform designed to treat calcified peripheral arterial disease (PAD). A press release notes that the acquisition will strengthen Stryker’s peripheral vascular portfolio by adding revascularisation technology.
