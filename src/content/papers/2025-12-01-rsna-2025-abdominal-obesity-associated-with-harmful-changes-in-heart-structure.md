## L’essentiel

A new study using advanced imaging found that abdominal obesity is associated with more harmful changes in heart structure than overall body weight alone, especially in men. The findings are being presented at the annual meeting of the Radiological Society of North America (RSNA) 2026 annual meeting (30 November–4 December, Chicago, USA).
