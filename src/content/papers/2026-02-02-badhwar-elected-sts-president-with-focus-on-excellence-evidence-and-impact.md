## L’essentiel

The Society of Thoracic Surgeons (STS) has elected Vinay Badhwar as its 62nd president. Badhwar, who is described by STS as an internationally recognised cardiothoracic surgeon, physician–scientist, and innovator, brings more than two decades of clinical, research, and administrative leadership experience to the presidency.
