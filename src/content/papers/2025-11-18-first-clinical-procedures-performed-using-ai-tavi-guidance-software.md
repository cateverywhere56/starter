## L’essentiel

Caranx Medical has announced the first clinical use of TAVIPILOT Soft, its artificial intelligence (AI)-driven intra-operative software which tracks real-time anatomical and instrument landmarks during transcatheter aortic valve implantation (TAVI). The first procedures were performed at Clinique Pasteur (Toulouse, France) by Didier Tchétché and his team as part of the SAITO-1A first-in-human trial.
