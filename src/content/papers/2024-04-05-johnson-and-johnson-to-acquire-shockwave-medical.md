## L’essentiel

Johnson &#38; Johnson is to acquire Shockwave Medical, it has been announced today. Under the terms of the transaction, Johnson &#38; Johnson will acquire all outstanding shares of Shockwave for US$335.00 per share in cash, corresponding to an enterprise value of approximately $13.1 billion including cash acquired.
