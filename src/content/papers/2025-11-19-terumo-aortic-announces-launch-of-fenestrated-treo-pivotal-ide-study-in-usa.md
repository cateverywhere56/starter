## L’essentiel

Terumo Aortic today announced enrolment of the first patient in the Fenestrated Treo pivotal investigational device exemption (IDE) study in the USA. This study is designed to evaluate the endovascular repair of juxtarenal and suprarenal aortic aneurysms using the Fenestrated Treo abdominal stent-graft system.
