## L’essentiel

Medtronic has announced it will exercise its option to acquire CathWorks. The intent to acquire CathWorks follows a 2022 strategic partnership with a co-promotion agreement for the CathWorks FFRangio system in the USA, Europe and Japan, where it is commercially available.
