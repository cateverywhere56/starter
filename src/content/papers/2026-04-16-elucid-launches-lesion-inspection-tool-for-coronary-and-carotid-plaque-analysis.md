## L’essentiel

Elucid has announced the commercial availability of a new Lesion Inspection Tool for coronary and carotid plaque analysis in its novel Plaque-IQ software suite. The tool provides physicians the ability to interrogate lesion-specific composition and plaque burden across any vessel, which can help the physician better understand the patient risk profile to enhance clinical decision-making.
