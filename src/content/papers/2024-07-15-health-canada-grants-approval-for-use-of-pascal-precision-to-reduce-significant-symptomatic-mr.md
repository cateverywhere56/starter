## L’essentiel

Edwards Lifesciences has announced Health Canada’s approval of the company&#8217;s Pascal Precision transcatheter valve repair system for the percutaneous reduction of significant, symptomatic mitral regurgitation (MR). “Patients suffering with debilitating symptoms due to symptomatic degenerative mitral regurgitation (DMR) represent a large and significantly underserved group,” said Neil Fam (St Michael&#8217;s Hospital, Toronto, Canada).
