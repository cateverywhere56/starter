## L’essentiel

Photon-counting detector computed tomography (CT) reduces the amount of contrast needed for CT angiography (CTA) while maintaining image quality, according to a new study published in Radiology: Cardiothoracic Imaging, a journal of the Radiological Society of North America (RSNA). The portion of the aorta that passes through the chest and abdomen is prone to aneurysms.
