## L’essentiel

MiRus has announced enrolment and treatment of the first patients in the STAR trial, a prospective, multicentre, randomised controlled trial studying the Siegel 8Fr aortic transcatheter heart valve (THV). The first procedures were performed at Piedmont Heart Institute by Pradeep Yadav and Vinod Thourani.
