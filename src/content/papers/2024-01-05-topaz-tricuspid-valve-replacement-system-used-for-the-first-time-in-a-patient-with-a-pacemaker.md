## L’essentiel

TRiCares SAS has announced the successful implantation of its Topaz transfemoral tricuspid heart valve replacement system in a patient with a pacemaker at the University Medical Center of Mainz (Mainz, Germany). Tricuspid regurgitation (TR) is a common and serious disease for which open heart surgery and symptomatic pharmacologic treatment are the current standard treatment options.
