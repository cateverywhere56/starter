## L’essentiel

Florian Enzmann (Innsbruck, Austria) speaks to Vascular News about PROMOTE ALI—a prospective, multicentre observational study focused on lower limb ischaemia. Acute limb ischaemia (ALI) is “not very well investigated,” the head of endovascular surgery at the Medical University Innsbruck states, adding that most of the available randomised studies are at least 20 years old.
