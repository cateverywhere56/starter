## L’essentiel

Caranx Medical has announced the successful completion of a robotic transcatheter aortic valve implantation (TAVI) procedure in an animal. The assisted heart valve implantation in the animal was successfully performed by the Stéphane Lopez (Institut Arnault Tzanck, Saint Laurent du var, France), alongside the company’s CMO, Eric Sejor, and CTO, Pierre Berthet-Rayne and Caranx team.
