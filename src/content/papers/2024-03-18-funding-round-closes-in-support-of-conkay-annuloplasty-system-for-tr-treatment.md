## L’essentiel

ConKay Medical Systems, a medtech start-up company developing a device for treating patients suffering from valvular regurgitation, has closed an oversubscribed US$1.8 million seed round. Multiple investment funds participated in the round including Unorthodox Ventures, SCP Ventures, WS Investment Company, and individual angel investors.
