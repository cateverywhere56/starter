## L’essentiel

Surmodics has announced enrolment of the first patient in PROWL, the Pounce thrombectomy system retrospective registry. PROWL is an open-label, retrospective, multicentre US registry of the Pounce system for the non-surgical removal of emboli and thrombi in the peripheral arterial vasculature.
