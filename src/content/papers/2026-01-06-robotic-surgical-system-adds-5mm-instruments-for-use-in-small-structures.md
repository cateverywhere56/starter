## L’essentiel

SS Innovations International has completed the development of five new 5mm surgical instruments for clinical use with its SSi Mantra system in procedures involving smaller anatomical structures. The newly developed 5mm surgical instruments include a spatula cautery, hook cautery, needle driver, bipolar forceps, and grasping forceps.
