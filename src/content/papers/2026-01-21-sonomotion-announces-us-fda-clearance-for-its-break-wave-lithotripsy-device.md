## L’essentiel

SonoMotion, a venture-backed medical device company developing non-invasive solutions for kidney stones, announced today that it received US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) 510(k) clearance for its Break Wave lithotripsy device. Break Wave uses low pressure-focused ultrasound to fragment kidney stones by creating standing stress waves within the stone under real-time ultrasound image guidance.
