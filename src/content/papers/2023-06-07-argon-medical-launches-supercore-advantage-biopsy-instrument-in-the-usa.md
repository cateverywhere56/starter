## L’essentiel

Argon Medical has announced the launch of the SuperCore Advantage semi-automatic biopsy instrument as the newest addition to its soft tissue biopsy product portfolio in the USA. The SuperCore Advantage is a disposable soft tissue biopsy instrument that provides a superior volume of tissue samples.
