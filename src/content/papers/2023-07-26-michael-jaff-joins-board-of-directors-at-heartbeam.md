## L’essentiel

HeartBeam, developer of a 3D-vector electrocardiogram (VECG) platform, has announced the appointment of Michael Jaff to its board of directors. Jaff is chief medical officer and vice president of clinical affairs, technology and innovation of the Peripheral Interventions division at Boston Scientific.
